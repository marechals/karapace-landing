  • All-in-One Inventory: Track insulin vials, pods, and sensors with simple, intuitive controls.
  • Personalized Alerts: Get notified before you run low — no more last-minute surprises.
  • iCloud Sync: Your data is always backed up and available on all your devices.
  • Easy Onboarding: Set up your inventory in minutes with our step-by-step wizard.
  • Privacy First: Your data stays on your device and your iCloud. We never collect or share your information.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?
Yes! DashCare does not collect or share your data. All information stays on your device and your iCloud account.

How do I get started?
Download DashCare from the App Store, follow the onboarding wizard, and you’re ready to go!

What devices are supported?
DashCare works on all modern iPhones and iPads running iOS 16 or later.
