Privacy Policy for DashCare

Effective Date: 1 July 2025

DashCare (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

DashCare does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All data you enter into the app (such as insulin, pods, sensors, and inventory information) is stored locally on your device and, if you enable iCloud, securely synced with your Apple iCloud account.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Your data is used solely to provide the app’s features, such as tracking your diabetes supplies, sending you alerts, and syncing your inventory across devices via iCloud.
  • We do not access, collect, or share your data with any third parties.

3. iCloud and Data Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your private iCloud account, managed by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

4. Notifications

If you enable notifications, DashCare will send you local notifications to remind you about your supply status. No notification data is sent to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

DashCare does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Children’s Privacy

DashCare is not intended for children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us here.
